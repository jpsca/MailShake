Mailshake

Simplify sending email from your Python app

from mailshake import SMTPMailer, EmailMessage

mailer = SMTPMailer(host=HOST, username=USER, password=PASSW)

email = EmailMessage(
    "Weekend getaway",
    "Here's a photo of us from our trip.",
    "from@example.com",
    to=['mary@example.com', 'bob@example.com'],
)
email.attach_file("picture.jpg")
mailer.send(email)

Mailshake is an elegant and simple email library for Python:

Available mailers:

  • SMTPMailer
  • AmazonSESMailer
  • ToConsoleMailer, prints the emails in the console, for development.
  • ToMemoryMailer, perfect for testing.
